Services

Screen Printing

Screen printing or silk screening is the most affordable and long lasting apparel printing application. We can imprint up to 6 colors per location. We use plastisol ink.

Embroidery

When it comes to creating that professional look, nothing does it quite like embroidery. We can stitch logos and custom designs on just about any garment.

Vinyl Heat Transfer

Vinyl transfers are the same as traditional screen printing. The difference is that we use a heat press to apply them to the garment.
